🛡️

WildPals does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information from children. Your child's privacy is our top priority.

📜 1. Introduction

WildPals is an educational game for children ages 4–6, developed by Mikhail Lesik (Spain). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our app. We designed WildPals from the ground up to protect children's privacy.

This policy applies to all versions of the WildPals app available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

✅ 2. Legal Compliance

WildPals is designed to comply with:

  • USA: Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), including the 2025 Final Rule effective April 22, 2026
  • EU: General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including special provisions for children's data
  • Apple: Kids Category guidelines and App Store Review Guidelines

We do NOT collect:

  • Names, usernames, or screen names
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Geolocation or precise location data
  • Advertising identifiers or device IDs transmitted to servers
  • Biometric data (Face ID, Touch ID, etc.)
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Browsing history or search history

📱 3. Local Data Storage

All data stays on the device. Nothing is sent to our servers.

  • Game progress: Unlocked animals, completed games, earned stars and badges — stored locally on device
  • Settings: Language, volume, selected profile — stored locally on device
  • Profile name: Parent-chosen display name (e.g., “Alex”), stored locally only, never transmitted
  • Anonymous random ID: A locally-generated identifier (e.g., anon_k3x8m1z) with no connection to real identity
  • Anonymous game events: Stored locally for parent dashboard display, automatically deleted after 30 days

🔧 4. Crash Reports (Sentry)

If a parent provides consent through the parental gate, anonymous crash reports may be sent to Sentry to help us fix bugs. These reports:

  • Contain no personally identifiable information (sendDefaultPii: false)
  • Do not include automatic session tracking (enableAutoSessionTracking: false)
  • Are automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Can be disabled at any time in parent settings

🔑 5. Persistent Identifiers (COPPA-Compliant)

WildPals uses two types of identifiers, both fully COPPA-compliant:

  • Anonymous device ID: A random string generated locally on the device. Not linked to any Apple or Google identifier. Never transmitted to any server. Never used for advertising or tracking.
  • Session ID: An ephemeral identifier regenerated on each app launch. Used only for local session duration tracking. Discarded when the app closes.

Neither identifier is ever transmitted off the device or used for advertising purposes.

🔗 6. Third-Party Services

RevenueCat

Used exclusively for subscription management. All purchase processing is handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). RevenueCat does not receive any child's personal information. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Sentry

Crash reporting service, activated only with parental consent. Configured with sendDefaultPii: false and enableAutoSessionTracking: false. No child PII is ever collected. Sentry Privacy Policy

What we do NOT use:

  • No advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No external analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No third-party tracking pixels or cookies

🔒 7. Parental Gate

A parental gate protects all sensitive areas of the app. To access settings, make purchases, or reach any external link, a parent or guardian must:

  • Press and hold a button for 3 seconds
  • Solve a simple math problem

This ensures that children cannot accidentally access settings, make purchases, or leave the app.

🔔 8. Push Notifications

WildPals uses local notifications only (scheduled on-device). These may include gentle play reminders. They are:

  • Scheduled entirely on the device — no server involved
  • Never used for advertising
  • Fully controllable and can be disabled in parent settings or device settings

🔐 9. Data Security

We take data security seriously, even though we store minimal data:

  • Encrypted storage: All local data stored via MMKV with device-level encryption
  • HTTPS-only: All network communication uses TLS encryption
  • App Transport Security (ATS): Enforced on iOS — no insecure connections allowed
  • No WebView: The app does not embed any web browser component, eliminating web-based tracking risks

🗑️ 10. Data Deletion

Parents can delete all of their child's data at any time:

  • In-app: Use the “Delete Child’s Data” button in parent settings (behind parental gate)
  • Uninstall: Removing the app deletes all locally stored data
  • Contact us: Email lesikom@gmail.com to request data deletion assistance

Since no data is stored on our servers, deletion is immediate and complete.

📄 11. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date at the top and notify parents through an in-app notice behind the parental gate. We will never reduce the level of privacy protection without obtaining verifiable parental consent.

  • Sin WebView: La aplicación no incorpora ningún componente de navegador web, eliminando riesgos de rastreo basado en la web

🗑️ 10. Eliminación de Datos

Los padres pueden eliminar todos los datos de su hijo en cualquier momento:

  • En la aplicación: Utilice el botón "Eliminar Datos del Niño" en la configuración para padres (detrás de la puerta parental)
  • Desinstalar: Al eliminar la aplicación se borran todos los datos almacenados localmente
  • Contáctenos: Envíe un correo a lesikom@gmail.com para solicitar asistencia en la eliminación de datos

Dado que no se almacenan datos en nuestros servidores, la eliminación es inmediata y completa.

📄 11. Cambios en esta Política

Si realizamos cambios sustanciales en esta Política de Privacidad, actualizaremos la fecha de vigencia en la parte superior y notificaremos a los padres mediante un aviso en la aplicación detrás de la puerta parental. Nunca reduciremos el nivel de protección de la privacidad sin obtener el consentimiento parental verificable.
